About this website

My accomplishments and personal information are shown on the website. Using the navigation bar, users may quickly visit a variety of sections on this website, including Home, Projects, Contacts, Video Demo, and Site Report. My picture is included in the header area along with a short bio that highlights my past experiences in school and present goals. Links to social networking platforms are also tastefully included.

Three projects are featured in the Projects section, complete with pictures, a synopsis, and links to Github for further information. There are several methods to reach me through the Contact area, such as a form for direct correspondence and links to my LinkedIn profile and email address. The website has an attractive and adaptable overall design. Media queries make ensuring that material is readable and aesthetically pleasing on both bigger displays and mobile devices. To sum up, I consider this website to be an excellent digital portfolio.
